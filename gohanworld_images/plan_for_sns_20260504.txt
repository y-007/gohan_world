1. Platform Reality (Audience + What Actually Works)
📘 Facebook

Audience behavior

Skews older (30–60+)
Passive consumption (scrolling, not searching)
Strong on community + relatability + trust

What converts

Short videos (Reels) are growing, but:
Story-based posts still outperform pure trends
Slightly longer attention span than IG

Minimum viable format

Video > Image
Hook in first 3 seconds
Subtitles REQUIRED (many watch muted)
CTA must feel practical, not salesy

Trend right now

“Relatable storytelling” (family, money, life struggles)
Slightly raw / non-polished content performs better than overproduced
📸 Instagram

Audience behavior

18–40
Visual-first, identity-driven
People follow for aspiration + emotion + aesthetics

What converts

Reels dominate reach (algorithm priority)
Carousel still strong for education/saves

Minimum viable format

Reel with:
Fast hook (0–2 sec)
Pattern interrupt
Text overlays
Clean visuals (this matters more than FB)

Trend right now

“Edutainment” (education + entertainment)
Quick cuts, captions, strong pacing
Micro-niche authority content (very important)
📌 Pinterest

Audience behavior

Search-based (VERY different)
Future-oriented (planning, ideas, solutions)
High buying intent

What converts

NOT traditional “social content”
Idea Pins / short videos tied to keywords

Minimum viable format

Vertical video OR static pin
SEO title + description is EVERYTHING
Content must solve a specific problem

Trend right now

Evergreen content > trending content
“How-to”, “step-by-step”, “best ways to…”
